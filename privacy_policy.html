Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

GojoCalories ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Camera and Image Data

GojoCalories requires access to your device's camera to enable the AI food logging feature.

  • Usage: When you take a photo of your food, the image is processed using our AI models to detect nutritional information (calories, macronutrients).
  • Processing: Images may be sent to our secure backend servers or third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini) for analysis.
  • Storage: We may store images to improve our AI models and provide you with a history of your food logs.

2. Personal Data

We collect information you provide during onboarding, such as:

  • Age, gender, height, and weight
  • Activity levels and dietary preferences
  • Email address (for account creation)

How We Use Your Information

We utilize the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To generate personalized nutrition plans.
  • To provide accurate calorie and macro tracking.
  • To improve our AI food recognition technology.
  • To sync with Apple Health or Google Fit (if enabled).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with carefully vetted third-party services (like AI providers or Stripe for payments) that are strictly necessary to provide the app's core features.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account, wipe all associated history, and remove your personal information at any time directly through the app settings.
